# Performance Review Card

Use this card after a WeChat article is published or after meaningful draft evaluation.

The goal is not only to record data.
The goal is to learn what writing moves actually worked.

## Record Basic Outcome

Capture at least:
- title
- article topic
- content pillar
- publish date
- read count
- like count
- share or forward signal if available
- comment quality if available

## Review The Article As Writing

Ask:
1. was the first screen strong enough to stop the right reader
2. what exact discomfort did the article hit first
3. what mechanism did the article successfully name
4. what core distinction carried the article
5. which line had the strongest propagation value
6. did the ending create self-questioning or only summary

## What To Keep

Capture:
- strongest opening move
- strongest scene or action detail
- strongest carry line
- strongest section transition
- strongest ending pressure move

## What To Fix

Capture:
- where the article became generic
- where it repeated instead of deepened
- where reader texture became thin
- where the ending softened too much

## Reuse Decision

For each article, decide:
- what to repeat in future writing
- what to avoid repeating
- whether the mechanism deserves a sequel topic
- whether the title pattern deserves reuse
