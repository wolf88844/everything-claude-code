# WeChat Atmosphere Reference Card

- a writing environment
- a distribution environment

This card is not about personal tone only.
It is about what kind of writing feels native to公众号 now.

## Core Judgment

公众号 is still a high-trust, long-form, cognition-heavy platform.
But it no longer behaves like a purely slow, subscriber-only environment.

It now rewards a combination of:
- depth
- first-screen competitiveness
- emotional recognition
- social shareability
- recommendation-readability for strangers

## Integrated Platform View

### 1. Depth Still Works

公众号 still supports:
- long-form essays
- structured thinking
- work and growth topics
- high-trust explanation
- save-worthy and share-worthy content

This means deep writing is still valid here.
But depth alone is no longer enough.

### 2. Distribution Is No Longer Pure Subscription

External ecosystem signals consistently suggest that公众号 now behaves as a hybrid system:
- subscription distribution
- recommendation distribution
- social amplification

This matters because your article is not only read by people who already know you.
It is also read by:
- recommendation readers
- friend-of-friend readers
- people encountering you through social signals

So the article must be readable without prior trust.

### 3. First Screen Matters Much More Than Before

公众号 still allows long-form reading.
But long-form does not mean slow warm-up.

The first screen should quickly do at least one of these:
- diagnose a familiar life state
- surface a hidden emotional truth
- name the misunderstanding the reader already lives inside

If the article opens with a clean thesis but no recognition, it will often feel cold.

### 4.公众号 Rewards Recognition Before Instruction

Readers may stay for depth.
But they usually enter because they feel recognized first.

This means platform-fit writing often moves like this:
1. this is what your life feels like
2. this is why you explain it this way
3. that explanation makes sense, but it is incomplete
4. here is the deeper frame

This is more native to公众号 than:
- polished thesis first
- framework first
- instruction first

### 5. Social Shareability Has Become More Important

公众号传播 is not only about read count.
It is also about whether the article contains lines people want to:
- save
- screenshot
- forward
- send to a friend with "这篇说得对"

Strong socially transmissible lines are usually:
- short
- emotionally accurate
- reality-based
- slightly relieving
- recognizably human

## What公众号 Atmosphere Favors

- articles that explain real life states
- writing that feels lived, not only correct
- slow unfolding of a wrong-but-attractive explanation
- judgment that comes through recognition
- a calm but penetrating tone
- enough realism that the reader sees their own day in it

## What公众号 Atmosphere Rejects More Easily

- slow warm-up with low relevance
- consultant-style correctness
- abstract concept articles with weak life scenes
- generic self-help motivation
- framework dumping too early
- copy that sounds optimized but not lived-in

## Decision Rules for Drafting

When drafting for公众号, ask:
1. Can a stranger understand the first screen without knowing me?
2. Does the article begin from life texture or only from my conclusion?
3. Does it explain why the reader believes the wrong thing?
4. Is there at least one line worth forwarding?
5. Would this feel native to微信里的阅读场景, not just to a blog?

If the answer is mostly no, the draft may be correct, but not yet公众号-native.

## Practical Writing Consequence

For broad work, career, burnout, or growth topics:
- start with life state
- then explain the reader's usual explanation
- then show why that explanation is attractive
- then reframe

For high-trust judgment topics:
- still begin with a recognizable state
- let authority appear through accuracy, not through abstract superiority
