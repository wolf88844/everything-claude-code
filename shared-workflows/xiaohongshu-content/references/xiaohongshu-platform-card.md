## Practical Rules For Writing

### Title
- default target: within 20 Chinese characters
- easier to understand than公众号 titles
- avoid long clause chains
- prefer one of these:
  - a familiar state
  - a hidden mistake
  - a useful result
  - a strong diagnosis

### Opening
The first 2 to 3 lines must quickly create one of these:
- recognition
- curiosity
- usefulness
- emotional accuracy

Do not warm up too slowly.

### Body
- short paras
- strong line breaks
- one main idea only
- easier to screenshot and save
- one note should feel lighter than a WeChat article

### Hashtags
Default: 3 to 5 hashtags.
Use a mix of:
- audience tag
- problem tag
- scene or category tag

Avoid:
- irrelevant trending tags
- too many broad tags
- tag spam

### Tone
Prefer:
- human
- sincere
- lightly conversational
- useful
- lived-in

Avoid:
- polished lecture tone
- consultant tone
- jargon-heavy abstraction
- aggressive clickbait that breaks trust

## Adaptation Heuristics For This User

This user's strongest公众号 writing traits are:
- hidden friction
- diagnosis
- reframe
- structural consequence

For Xiaohongshu, keep the first three and lighten the fourth.

So the preferred adaptation pattern is:
1. familiar scene
2. sharp diagnosis
3. new understanding
4. lighter takeaway

Instead of:
1. long buildup
2. deep explanation
3. structural escalation
4. heavy close

## Quick Test

A draft is more Xiaohongshu-native if:
- the title can be understood in one glance
- the opening can be screenshotted without context
- the body feels like one useful note, not a compressed essay
- the hashtags help search and recommendation, not just decoration
